Information

This is a Δv planner for the game Kerbal Space Program developed by Squad.

Low orbits are 10 km above atmosphere or terrain obstacles.
All values are measured as vacuum Δv in m/s.
When two values are given, minimum value includes no plane change Δv, maximum value includes maximum plane change Δv.
Atmospheric ascent values are typical.
More/less efficient ascents are likely, depending on ascent profile, TWR and aerodynamic effects.
Flight time is based on 10 years of average flight possibilities simulated on AlexMoon's Launch Window Planner, from day 1.
